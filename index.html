Gulam Jamun

Ingredients

  1. Milk Solids : You’ll need either 1 cup of full-fat milk powder1 or 1 cup of Khoya .
  2. Flour and Leavening Agent : Prepare 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour and a pinch of baking soda.
  3. Fat and Liquid : Have 1 tablespoon of ghee and 4 tablespoons of milk ready.
  4. Flavorings : Lastly, you’ll need 4 green cardamom pods2 and 1 teaspoon of rose water.

Recipe

  1. Prepare the Dough: Combine milk powder, Bisquick, and melted butter. Gradually add whole milk to the dry ingredients until the dough becomes firm
  2. Shape the Dough: Divide the dough into 20 portions and roll each portion into a ball.
  3. Fry the Dough Balls: Heat oil on high. When it’s hot, reduce the heat to low to medium and deep fry the dough balls.
  4. Prepare and Soak in Syrup: Heat sugar, water, cardamom, saffron, and rosewater in a pan for 5 minutes. Once the syrup is done, set it aside and soak the fried dough balls in it.

Rava Barfi

Ingredients

  1. Semolina (Rava): You’ll need 1 cup of semolina, also known as rava.
  2. Clarified Butter (Ghee): Prepare ¾ cup of clarified butter, commonly known as ghee. Sugar: Have 1 cup of sugar ready.
  3. Milk and Cardamom Powder: Lastly, you’ll need 1 cup of milk and 1 tsp of cardamom powder.

Recipe

  1. Preparation: Heat the ghee in a pan, add the rava and mix it well. Fry for 2 mins on a low to medium heat.
  2. Adding Ingredients: Then, add the sugar.
  3. Cooking: Pour the milk and mix everything well until the sugar melts and the mixture turns gooey. Keep stirring until the mixture thickens and begins to leave the pan slightly.
  4. Final Touch: Transfer the mixture to a greased tray, press down immediately with a spatula, and allow it to cool down. Cut into pieces when it’s still hot but not very hot.
